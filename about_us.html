Hiring, guided by AI.
Decisions made by humans.

Our mission is to make hiring fair, fast, and scalable.

SorsX exists to remove friction from high-volume hiring by automating screening and
interviews with AI - so every candidate is evaluated objectively, and hiring teams can
focus on choosing the right people.

A better way to evaluate talent

When hundreds or thousands of candidates apply, traditional hiring processes can’t keep up. Recruiters are pushed to rush screenings, rely on resumes alone, and spend hours coordinating interviews across time zones - causing qualified candidates to be overlooked.

SorsX was built to change this. Our platform acts as a navigation system for hiring, guiding teams smoothly from application to shortlist by replacing friction with structure, speed, and fairness.
